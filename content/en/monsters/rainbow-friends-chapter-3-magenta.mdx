<Section icon={Swords} title="Magenta and the New Monsters">

Understanding the mechanics of **rainbow friends chapter 3 magenta** is essential for survival. Magenta himself operates with a level of aggression that dwarfs Cyan. If he catches you moving in your box, or even if he simply sees you enter it, the box becomes useless. He will actively break through lockers and small holes to reach you, making traditional hiding spots death traps.

Alongside Magenta, Teal introduces a stealth element. Teal is nearly invisible, with only his eyes glowing faintly. His footsteps are almost silent, requiring players to rely on the new Camera mechanic to stun him and force him into a visible state.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Objective Walkthrough">

To survive **rainbow friends chapter 3 magenta**, players must complete a series of complex tasks while avoiding the growing roster of monsters. The difficulty spikes significantly during the mid-game when the power is cut, and you are forced to navigate in total darkness.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fix the Broken Wires">
    Explore the main Warehouse area to find and fix electrical wires. This powers the main hub but inadvertently releases the new Rainbow Friends from their cages. Watch out for the original five monsters who are active from the start.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Fuses & Evade Magenta">
    With Magenta, Teal, and Orange now active, you must collect fuses to repair the train. Magenta will begin breaking hiding spots during this phase, so stay mobile and use the environment to break line of sight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Daycare Rescue Mission">
    Board the train to the Daycare. Here, you must find Lookies and transport them to the Metal Hatch. Pink is the sole monster here; she will flip your box if you hide for more than a few seconds in her radius.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Generator Restoration">
    Return to the Warehouse. Magenta has cut the power, leaving you in darkness. Locate and activate all Generators. Use the glowing eyes of the monsters to track their movements. Indigo and Violet make their debut here.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Rejected Friends Maze">

Objective 6 is widely considered the hardest challenge in the series. Red takes players to a secret underground facility housing the "Rejected Friends"—monsters deemed too unstable or "dull" for the public. This area is a literal maze where traditional survival rules are thrown out the window.

The Rejected Friends include Brown, Grey, White, and Black. Each requires a specific, often counter-intuitive reaction to survive. For example, White functions similarly to Cyan, but his vision is entirely movement-based; you don't even need a box if you stand perfectly still.

| Rejected Friend | Mechanic | How to Survive |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Brown** | Charges when you stay still | Flash him when he charges; maintain eye contact |
| **Grey** | Slow but ignores boxes | Simply outrun him; he cannot be hidden from |
| **White** | Movement-based vision | Stand perfectly still when he is nearby |
| **Black** | Requires distraction | Use a Fire Torch then flash 3 times with Camera |
