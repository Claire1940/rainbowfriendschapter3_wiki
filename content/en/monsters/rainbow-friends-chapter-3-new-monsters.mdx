<Section icon={Swords} title="Primary New Monsters: Magenta, Teal, and Pink">

The core of the new experience revolves around three major additions that change how players interact with the environment. Unlike the original cast, these monsters have advanced AI that punishes traditional hiding strategies.

| Monster | Primary Mechanic | Survival Strategy | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Magenta** | Breaks lockers/hiding spots | Avoid line of sight entirely | ★★★★★ |
| **Teal** | Invisible/Camouflage | Use Camera flash to reveal | ★★★★☆ |
| **Pink** | Flips player boxes | Do not stay in box near her | ★★★★☆ |

**Magenta: The Main Antagonist**
Magenta acts as the central villain of this chapter. He is highly intelligent and can detect players even when they are hidden in lockers or small holes. If Magenta catches you moving into a box, that box becomes permanently useless against him until the final stages of the game.

**Teal: The Chameleon**
Teal utilizes a camouflage ability, making him nearly invisible except for his glowing eyes. His footsteps are almost silent, requiring players to listen for faint environmental cues. To counter Teal, you must use the new **Camera** item to stun him, which forces him to remain visible for a short duration.

**Pink: The Box Hunter**
Found primarily in the Daycare, Pink subverts the "hide in the box" mechanic. If you remain in your box within her radius for too long, she will flip the box over and initiate a high-speed chase. Unlike other monsters, you can move in your box around her, but you must never stay stationary.

<Callout type="tip" title="Camera Management">
The Camera has a significant cooldown. You only get three flashes before it needs to recharge, so save your flashes for Teal or the Rejected Friends.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Survival Guide for the Rejected Friends">

Objective 6 introduces the most difficult segment in the franchise, taking place in a scary underground maze filled with the "Rejected Friends." These are monsters Red deemed too unstable or "ugly" for public display.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Countering Brown">
    Brown will charge at you if you remain still. You must stare directly at Brown to prevent his charge, similar to "Weeping Angel" mechanics. If he charges, use the Camera flash immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Distracting Black">
    Black is nearly invincible unless distracted. You must find a **Fire Torch** item and place it. Once Black is distracted by the flame, you must flash him three times with the Camera to temporarily banish him.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Evading Grey">
    Grey is a slow but persistent stalker. Boxes do not work against him. Because he is slow, the best strategy is to simply maintain distance and never get cornered in the maze.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Avoiding White">
    White functions similarly to Cyan but relies entirely on movement detection. You do not need a box to survive White; you simply need to stand perfectly still when he is looking in your direction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Rejected Friend | Movement Type | Box Utility | Counter Item |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Brown** | Charging | Ineffective | Camera / Eye Contact |
| **Black** | Stalking | Ineffective | Fire Torch + Camera |
| **Grey** | Walking | Ineffective | Distance / Speed |
| **White** | Visual | Ineffective | Standing Still |

<Callout type="success" title="Maze Tip">
The Rejected Friends level is a literal maze. Always keep a mental map of the Fire Torch spawn locations, as you will need them to deal with Black multiple times.
</Callout>

</Section>
