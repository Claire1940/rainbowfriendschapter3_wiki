<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for Chapter 3">

Surviving **rainbow friends chapter 3 green** requires a different mindset than dealing with Blue or Orange. Because Green is blind, you do not need to hide behind walls to break line-of-sight. Instead, you must manage your physical space and timing.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Search Pattern">
    Watch Green from a distance. He will walk a few steps, stop, and swing his arms. Memorize these "stop zones" and avoid standing in them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Under-Arm Maneuver">
    When Green is sensing the area, he often raises one arm while the other sweeps low. It is possible to safely walk directly under his raised arm if you time it correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Crouch Spaces">
    If Green is approaching a narrow hallway, duck into a locker or a crouch space. Green cannot reach into these areas, making them 100% safe zones regardless of his proximity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Listen for Footsteps">
    Green has distinct, heavy footstep sounds. Use high-quality audio or headphones to track his position through walls, allowing you to move to the opposite side of his path.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
During the "Pause" window after Green finishes sensing an area, his hitbox briefly becomes inactive. Experienced players can actually walk *through* his character model during this split second to escape a corner.
</Callout>
