<Section icon={Target} title="Survival Steps for Purple Encounters">

Surviving an encounter with **rainbow friends chapter 3 purple** requires a combination of audio awareness and environmental interaction. Whether you are dealing with the leaking vents of the past or the advanced environmental hazards of the present, these steps remain the gold standard for survival.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Zone">
    Before entering a room, look for leaking water or thick purple mist. If you hear metallic thumping, Purple is currently repositioning within the vents.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Safe Paths">
    In vent areas, look for white footprints on the pipes. These indicate the only safe path where Purple cannot reach you. In fog areas, identify the large rocks protruding from the ground.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Your Stance">
    If you must enter the fog, crouch immediately. Crouching acts as a stealth mechanic that prevents Purple from grabbing you, though it will still follow you closely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Escape">
    Move quickly to the nearest elevation (rock or pipe). Once you are on a different elevation level, Purple will typically retreat to a corner and vanish temporarily.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
When exiting the mist, always do so on flat ground. Changing elevation while transitioning out of the fog can glitch the "safe zone" detection, allowing Purple to grab you at the last second.
</Callout>

</Section>
