<Section icon={Shield} title="Rainbow Friends Chapter 3 Blue: New Mechanics">

The most significant change in **rainbow friends chapter 3 blue** involves the behavior of the titular character, Blue. While Blue remains the primary antagonist appearing from Night 1, his AI has been significantly upgraded to counter veteran players who rely solely on the box mechanic. Blue is now faster and possesses a "Dash" ability that allows him to close distances instantly.

When Blue is about to dash, he will stop momentarily and look at the ground. This is your only window to find cover that isn't just a box. If a "!" indicator appears above your head while you are inside a box, it means Blue has detected you and his dash attack will result in an immediate game over.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Do not rely on your box if you see the "!" indicator. The box only works if you are hidden *before* Blue enters your immediate line of sight. In Chapter 3, his detection radius is wider than ever.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Survival Guide for Odd Fields">

Navigating the forest of **rainbow friends chapter 3 blue** requires a methodical approach. The map is designed to be more open than previous chapters, offering more paths but fewer hard-wall hiding spots.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Dash Timing">
    When encountering Blue, watch his posture. If he looks down, he is charging a dash. Pivot behind a tree or a rock immediately. Do not hide in your box if he is already looking at you, as the "!" mark will seal your fate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage the Food Dispenser">
    Orange's line is now invisible. To slow him down, locate the food dispenser near the start of his path. Keeping this dispenser full will make Orange "slower" as he becomes satiated, giving you more time to complete objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize the Environment">
    Unlike the Factory, the Forest has interactive elements like leaf piles and waterfalls. Use leaf piles to cushion your fall if Yellow grabs you, and avoid the river if you hear Cyan's splashing sounds nearby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate the Web Zones">
    Brown's webs are often placed in narrow chokepoints. Watch the ground carefully. If you are stunned, you have a 5-second window where you are completely vulnerable to any nearby Rainbow Friend.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Map Mechanic">
The "Odd World" portal is your ultimate goal. To activate it, you must collect specific items scattered across the forest, similar to the blocks and fuses in previous chapters.
</Callout>
