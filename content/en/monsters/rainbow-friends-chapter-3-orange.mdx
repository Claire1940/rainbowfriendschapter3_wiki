Orange is a major antagonist in the series, characterized by its distinct appearance and terrifying physical capabilities. Resembling a scrawny Baryonyx, Orange features bright orange skin, bulging eyes with vertical pupils that occasionally look in opposite directions, and rows of sharp, conical teeth lining its triangular jaws. Unlike other monsters that wander aimlessly, Orange operates on a highly structured behavioral pattern. Understanding its physical traits is essential for survival, as it is the fastest monster in the game and possesses no warning buffer when catching players.

<Section icon={Map} title="The Core Mechanics: Rampage and Food Dispenser 3000">

During standard gameplay, Orange spends the vast majority of its time resting inside its dark hideout. However, this passive state is entirely dependent on its feeding cycle. Below the resting monster lies a food bowl that slowly drains over a period of 1 minute and 40 seconds. When the bowl empties, Orange enters an enraged state, triggering a loud alarm and initiating a map-wide rampage.

During a rampage, Orange follows a fixed path marked by a glowing, jagged orange line. It travels along this path for approximately 45 seconds before returning to its cave. If a player crosses this line or enters Orange's field of vision, Orange will instantly break its path to chase them.

| Mechanic | Time/Distance | Player Action Required |
| **Food Depletion** | 1 minute and 40 seconds | Pull lever at Food Dispenser 3000 |
| **Rampage Duration** | Approximately 45 seconds | Hide in a box or locker immediately |
| **Trigger Distance** | Inner circle (Bells zone) | Move quickly; do not linger near bells |
| **Leave Trigger** | Outer circle boundary | Exit the zone to reset Orange's alert state |

To prevent these dangerous rampages, players must coordinate to keep Orange fed. This is achieved by utilizing the Food Dispenser 3000 located near its lair.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Food Bowl">
    Keep a close eye on the food levels in Orange's cave. Try to coordinate with teammates to ensure someone is always near the Food Dispenser 3000 when the timer nears depletion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cross the Bells Wisely">
    Approach the Food Dispenser 3000. Be aware that the floor surrounding the machine is rigged with bells. Stepping on them causes Orange to lift its head, entering an alert state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Pull the Lever">
    Run directly into the lever on the side of the Food Dispenser 3000. This activates the machine, dropping fresh food packs and fully replenishing the food bowl.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evacuate the Zone">
    Immediately move away from the cave. If you linger within the "Leave Trigger Distance" for too long, Orange will exit its cave to investigate, initiating a short-distance chase even if the food bowl is full.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
