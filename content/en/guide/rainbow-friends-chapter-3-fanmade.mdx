<Section icon={Swords} title="Monster Guide and Combat Tactics">

While you cannot "fight" the monsters in the traditional sense, defeating them in **rainbow friends chapter 3** involves using their own mechanics against them. The fan-made version introduces "Gold" as a lingering threat from the previous chapter, though the primary focus remains on the core cast.

| Monster | Primary Weakness | Detection Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blue** | Lockers / Boxes | Line of Sight |
| **Green** | Standing Still | Sound / Touch |
| **Orange** | Staying off the Path | Motion Sensors |
| **Purple** | Avoiding Vents/Water | Environmental Cues |

To survive the school environment, you must master the "crouch-walk" technique. In the library and science labs, Green is particularly dangerous because the narrow aisles make it easy to accidentally bump into him. If you see Blue, do not run in a straight line; use the classroom doors to break his line of sight and hide under a desk immediately.

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
When Orange is active, listen for the siren sound. This indicates he is about to charge through the hallways. If you are not near a locker, find a side room and stay away from the orange trail on the floor.
</Callout>
