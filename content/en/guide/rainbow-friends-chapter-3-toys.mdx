<Section icon={Swords} title="Poseable Action Figures and Collectible Mini Figures">

Action figures and mini figures form the backbone of any Rainbow Friends collection. The action figures stand at 5 inches tall, featuring multiple points of articulation that allow players to recreate iconic jumpscares and gameplay moments. Each 5-inch figure comes packaged with a specific in-game item, such as a building block or a food pack, adding extra play value.

On the other hand, the 2.5-inch mini figures are designed primarily for display. While they lack poseability, they make up for it with unique poses, high-density plastic construction, and rare chase variants. Collectors can hunt for neon, metallic, and sparkly clear finishes that are randomly inserted into blind bags and mystery boxes.

<Callout type="warning" title="Anti-Counterfeit Alert">
Bootleg action figures often have loose joints, mismatched paint colors, and lack the official accessories. Genuine figures have a sturdy build and precise facial details, particularly around Blue's crown and drool.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Unboxing Guide: Head Bundles and Mystery Playsets">

If you are looking for the ultimate unboxing experience, the giant character Head Bundles are the top recommendation. These large plastic storage containers are shaped like the heads of iconic monsters (such as Blue, Green, Yellow, or Cyan) and open up to reveal a treasure trove of exclusive merchandise. Inside, collectors can find exclusive action figures, mini figures, plushies, stickers, and even roleplay items like Blue's crown or eyepatch.

Because these bundles contain mystery items, approaching the unboxing strategically can help you maximize your collection while avoiding duplicates.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Buying Strategy">
The items inside the Head Bundles often feature minor color swaps or metallic paint finishes that are not available in individual packs. Keep these exclusive variants separate to retain their collector value.
</Callout>

Follow these steps to get the most out of your mystery playset unboxing:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check the Packaging Seal">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unveil the Microfigures">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Discover Wearable Accessories">
