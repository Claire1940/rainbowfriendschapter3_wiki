<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Parental Controls on Roblox">

Roblox provides a robust suite of parental controls that allow you to customize your child's gaming environment. By configuring these settings, you can ensure that your child is only exposed to age-appropriate experiences and safe communication channels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Account PIN">
    Navigate to the Roblox Settings menu, select the Security tab, and toggle on the Account PIN option. Create a secure 4-digit PIN that only you know. This prevents your child from altering any safety configurations you establish.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Restrict Communication Settings">
    Go to the Privacy tab in the Settings menu. Locate the communication settings and set 'Who can chat with me in app' and 'Who can chat with me' to 'No one' or 'Friends'. This blocks direct messages and public chat from strangers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Configure Content Maturity Limits">
    Under the Parental Controls tab, locate the Allowed Experiences section. Set the maximum age threshold to match your child's maturity level. Restricting this to age-appropriate tiers will automatically block access to games rated above the limit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Set Spending Restrictions">
    Within the same Parental Controls tab, configure monthly spending limits or completely disable the ability to purchase Robux. This protects your connected credit cards or payment accounts from accidental or unauthorized transactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Settings Secured">
Once these steps are completed, your child can enjoy the multiplayer challenges of the game without exposure to unmoderated chat or sudden financial charges.
</Callout>

</Section>
